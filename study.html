Schools on Wheels is a nonprofit organizations that provides tutoring services to homeless children in grades kindergarten through twelve. Anyone can volunteer to tutor homeless students. Today, the program has hundreds of volunteers each year. School on Wheels is located in several locations across Southern California. Click here to check out their website!

Hannah Shulman, one of the creators of this website, created an app based off of the popular card game "Time's Up!". After playing the game, Hannah noticed that she had memorized the words from the game after playing with those cards for three rounds. She thought this would be a great way to study for a test. This app offers study sets for the SAT Vocabulary words, AP Psychology words, and AP European History words. She hopes this app turns "studying" into a fun process, and allows friends to not only study together, but to also bond in the process. Click here to view the app!
