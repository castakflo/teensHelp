Food insecurity is a major worldwide problem. 842 Million people worldwide don't have enough to eat. The vast majority of hungry people live in developing countries. However, in the United States, 1 in 8 Americans were food insecure, including 13 million chidren. Food insecurity disproportionally affects people of colour, with 1 in 4 African American children, and 1 in 4 Latinx children consistently struggling with hunger. Combatting hunger is even harder for these groups, as poverty rates for African Americans was more than twice that of white people. Click here for more information on how you can get involved!
