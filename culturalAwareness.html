CULTURAL AWARENESS!


As it becomes crucial to understand the uncharted experiences and circumstances of others it is imperative to build human connection across the world. The internet can be a usful resource. Let's make this happen!!!



1. Make a Culture Club in your school or community!!

Organizing a club can allow for the youth to express what experiences and challenges they have had to overcome.


2. Hold conversations with people you may not know a lot about.

Speaking with people that have different cultural backgrounds can help you and others become more aware.


3. Encourage diversity in groups and public places.

Thinking about how different types of people might experience a setting differently can build a comprehension of cultural differences. Knowing this might help you and/or others solve them.


4. Stand by those who face discrimination.

Advocating for those who face discrimination not only shows others that you are an ally, but can make people feel more comfortable sharing their stories and culture with others.
