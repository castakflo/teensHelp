Cultural Awareness


As it becomes crucial to understand the uncharted experiences and circumstances of others it is imperative to build human connection across the world. The internet can be a usful resource. Let's make this happen!!!



1. Make A Culture club in you school or community!!

This can allow for the youth to express what experiences they have undergone.


2. Hold Conversations With People You may Not Know A Lot About.

Speaking with others can help you and others become more culturally aware.


3. Encourage Diversity in groups and public places.

Thinking About the different types of people in a setting can build a comprehension of cultual differences and how to work through them.


4. Stand By Those who face discrimination.

Advocating for those who face discrimination not only shows others that you are an ally but can make them feel more comfortable sharing their stories and culture with others.
